Welcome to EdCamps!

About Us

EdCamps is a fun, week-long childrens' camp for children in grades 1-8 in the Bay Area. At EdCamps, your child will experience a variety of games, sports, and hands-on educational activites. Your child will be under the supervision of our enthusiastic staffers, who cannot wait to meet your child. Sign up today to find the closest EdCamps location to you!
